Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points?

Complete tasks like watching highlight videos, referring friends (max 100), daily check-ins, subscribing to our YouTube channel, and engaging with community content.

How many friends can I refer?

Each user can refer a maximum of 100 friends. You earn 50 points for each successful referral.

Do points expire?

No, your points never expire. You can save them for big prizes or redeem smaller rewards anytime.

How does the leaderboard work?

The leaderboard ranks the top 1000 users by total points earned. It updates in real-time as users complete tasks.

How do I redeem prizes?

Visit the Prizes page, select a prize you have enough points for, and click "Redeem". You'll receive a confirmation email with delivery details.

Is this affiliated with FIFA?

No. Global Goal 2026 is an independent fan community platform and is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by FIFA.
