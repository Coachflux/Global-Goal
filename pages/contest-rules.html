Contest Rules

These rules govern the Global Goal 2026 points program and prize redemptions.

1. Eligibility

Open to individuals aged 13 and older worldwide. Void where prohibited by law.

2. How to Earn Points

Points are earned by completing verified tasks including watching videos, referring friends (max 100), daily check-ins, and social media engagement.

3. Prize Redemption

Prizes can be redeemed once sufficient points are accumulated. All redemptions are final. Prizes are subject to availability and shipping restrictions.

4. Leaderboard

The leaderboard displays the top 1000 users ranked by total points earned. Rankings are updated in real-time.

5. Fair Play

Any attempt to manipulate the system, use bots, or create fake accounts will result in disqualification and account termination.
